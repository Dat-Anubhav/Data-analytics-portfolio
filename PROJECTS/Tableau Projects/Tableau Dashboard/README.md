UPI Transactions: Interactive Financial Dashboard

Project Overview

This project analyzes a dataset of digital payments to uncover consumer spending habits, regional adoption of UPI, and the performance of various banking institutions and merchants. The final dashboard was built in Tableau to provide an interactive exploratory experience.

Tech Stack
    
    •	Visualization Tool: Tableau Public
    
    •	Data Processing: Microsoft Excel
    
    •	Key Metrics: Transaction Volume, Success Rate, Regional Distribution, Merchant Frequency.

Key Analytical Insights

  1. Regional Performance

    •	Top Cities: Identified Delhi (₹5.03M) and Bangalore (₹4.87M) as the primary drivers of transaction value.
    
    •	Global Reach: Analyzed cross-border currency transactions involving USD, EUR, and GBP, reflecting the international versatility of the platform.

  2. Merchant & Payment Trends

    •	Top Merchants: Financial activity is heavily concentrated on Amazon, Flipkart, and Swiggy, showcasing a preference for e-commerce and food delivery.

    •	Payment Methods: Evaluated the split between QR Code, UPI ID, and Phone Number payments, confirming a high reliance on mobile-first scanning technologies.

  3. Demographic & Behavioral Data

    •	Age Group Analysis: Segmented transactions into distinct age brackets to understand which generation is driving digital payment growth.

    •	Device Usage: Noted a dominant preference for Mobile transactions over Laptop/Tablet, emphasizing the importance of mobile-optimized payment interfaces.
