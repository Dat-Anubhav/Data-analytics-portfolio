EuroHub Store: Sales & Trend Analysis

Project Overview

This project provides a deep dive into the sales performance of EuroHub Store. By analyzing transaction history, product margins, and seasonal trends, this dashboard offers data-driven recommendations to maximize profitability and optimize stock levels.
Tech Stack
  
  •	Tool: Power BI Desktop
  
  •	Data Source: Excel (Relational datasets: Customers, Products, Promotions, and Sales)
  
  •	Analysis Focus: Sales Trends, Product Profitability, and Discount Impact.

Key Insights
  
  1. Product Performance

      •	Top Performer: The Apple iPhone 14 leads in both sales volume and total profit contribution.

      •	Volume Leader: Zara Casual Shirts achieved the highest total number of units sold.

      •	Inventory Optimization: Identified that items like the Borosil Glass Set and Wildcraft Bags contribute less than 1% to total profit, suggesting a "Just-in-Time" ordering approach to save warehouse space.

  2. Time-Series & Seasonal Trends

      •	Peak Season: Consistent high sales in Quarter 4, specifically during December and January.

      •	Off-Season: Identified recurring sales dips in February, April, and November, providing a window for targeted promotional campaigns.

      •	Correlation: Confirmed a direct linear correlation between Net Sales Amount and Total Profit across 90% of the product catalog.

  3. Discount & Marketing Analysis

      •	High-Impact Discounts: Analyzed the Titan Wrist Watch, which maintained high profitability despite significant discount percentages.

      •	Strategic Recommendations: * Implement regional digital marketing for high-margin products.
o	Use "Loss Leader" pricing on daily essentials (like toothpaste) to maintain steady foot traffic.
