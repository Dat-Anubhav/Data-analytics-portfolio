E-Commerce Sales Performance Dashboard

A comprehensive data visualization project built with Metabase to analyze and track key performance indicators (KPIs) for a multi-year e-commerce dataset. This dashboard provides a high-level overview of revenue trends, product distribution, and goal tracking.

Dashboard Overview:-

The Orders Overview dashboard transforms raw transactional data into actionable business intelligence. It monitors $1.5M+ in total revenue and evaluates performance against a $1M growth target.

Key Metrics and KPIs:-

    •	Total Revenue: $1,510,621.68 (Exceeded $1,000,000.00 goal).

    •	Monthly Performance: Real-time tracking of revenue (e.g., $30,759.47 for April 2026).

    •	Sales Growth: Visualized year-over-year growth from 2022 through early 2026, peaking in 2025 at $577,067.05.

Features
  
    •	Product Category Breakdown: Analysis of revenue distribution across core categories:
      
      (i)	Widgets: 28.0%
      
      (ii)	Gadgets: 26.9%

      (iii)	Gizmos: 25.4%
      
      (iv) Doohickeys: 19.7%

    •	Temporal Analysis: Visualizations showing product category performance grouped by year.
    
    •	Order Trends: Dual-axis visualization comparing the total count of orders against the sum of revenue per month to identify seasonal trends.

    •	Geospatial Insights: Integration of customer location data to understand regional sales distribution.

Technical Stack
    
    •	Business Intelligence Tool: Metabase
    
    •	Database: Standardized E-commerce Dataset (Metabase Sample Data)

    •	Environment: Localhost deployment

    •	Querying: SQL and Metabase GUI Query Builder

Key Insights
  
  1.	Goal Attainment: The business successfully surpassed its $1M revenue milestone during the 2024-2025 period.

  2.	Category Leadership: Widgets served as the leading revenue driver, contributing over $423,000 to the total revenue.

  3.	Growth Trajectory: Recorded a significant upward trend in sales volume between 2023 and 2025, nearly tripling annual revenue within a two-year window.
